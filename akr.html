CERTIFICATE AND REGISTRATIONS

The good old days are back!!! every place is unique and is special for some of the other cultural, traditional or fantasy reasons. kovilpatti –a place in thoothukudi district of tamil nadu, india. the state with tradition and rich in culture also maintains its love for kovilpatti kadalaimittai or chikki; in other words, it is called as peanut candy. peanut candies are a special and all-time favorite to a majority of the people irrespective of age groups till now.
But what makes it so special???
peanut candy is made healthy and tasty with good quality roasted peanut, jaggery, and glucose. absolutely no added white sugar and preservatives.this candy is a protein-rich sweet cum snack which was once the star of the snack pack and slowly the trend moved down to junk foods and other modern-day snacks
AKR brothers from the land of historical culture and heritage had made up their mind to take not only their people but rest of the world to few years down the lane and serve them the best of the best healthy yet yummy-peanut candies.
mr. marichamy and mr.manisankar sons of mr. ramasubbu from avalnatham village –both arefounders of akr healthy foods, had started their passion cum business from the root on 27th april 2018. the manufacturing of akr healthy foods is set up at heart of kovilpatti, tamil nadu. the place once known for its homemade candy is again getting into a track with lots of other varieties of candies.

AKR healthy foods are all set to serve the rest of the countries the taste of homemade candies. the akr products are is proven healthier, hygienic and certified by sgs.
